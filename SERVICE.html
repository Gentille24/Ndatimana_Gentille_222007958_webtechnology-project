Our Services

Menu Management:

Ability for restaurants to upload and manage their menus. Options for categorizing items, adding descriptions, and specifying prices. Support for images to showcase food items..

Customer Support:

Help desk or chat support for assisting users with inquiries, order tracking, and issue resolution. Knowledge base or FAQ section to address common questions and concerns.

Feedback and Reviews:

.Option for customers to leave reviews and ratings for restaurants and dishes. .Feedback mechanism to collect input on the overall ordering experience. .Moderation tools to manage reviews and address any issues.

Order Management:

.Dashboard for restaurant staff to view and manage incoming orders. .Notification system to alert staff of new orders. .Ability to update order status (e.g., confirmed, preparing, dispatched).
